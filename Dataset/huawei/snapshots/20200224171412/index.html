Huawei EMUI/Magic UI security updates

About EMUI/Magic UI Security Patch Updates

  • Huawei will introduce a monthly and quarterly security patch update programme for the following device models.
  • The monthly and quarterly security patch update programmes include patches for the Android OS, and patches for Huawei devices released by Huawei.
  • The release time of security patches may vary depending on the region and device model.
  • The last security update for M3 Lite(CPN-L0J) was released in December 2019, with the Android security patch level of 2019-08-01.

Current monthly security patch updates will be released for the following device models¹

HUAWEI P series: P30 Pro, P30, P20 Pro, P20

HUAWEI Mate series: Mate 20 X, Mate 20 Pro, Mate 20, Mate 20 RS

HONOR series: HONOR 20, HONOR 20 PRO, Honor View20, Honor 10, Honor View10

Current quarterly security patch updates will be released for the following device models²

HUAWEI P series: P30 lite, P20 lite, P Smart 2019, P 9 lite mini, P Smart

HUAWEI Mate series: Mate 20 Lite, Mate 10 Lite

HUAWEI Y series: Y9 Prime 2019, Y9 2019, Y9 2018, Y6 2019, Y6 Pro 2019, Y6 2018, Y5 2019, Y5 2018, Y5 Prime 2018, Y5 Lite, Y5 Lite 2018, Y3 2018, Y MAX,HUAWEI Y9s,HUAWEI Y6s

HONOR series: Honor 20i, Honor 10i, Honor 10 Lite, Honor 9 Lite, Honor 9N, Honor 9i, Honor Play, Honor 8A, Honor 8X, Honor 8S, Honor 8C, Honor 9X, Honor 7S

HUAWEI nova series: nova 5T, nova 4, nova 3, nova 3e, nova Lite 3,nova 3i

Tablets: M5 (8.4" & 10.8"), M5 Pro, T5 (10.1"), M5 lite (10.1" & 8.0")

¹The number of device models with monthly security patch updates is subject to change. Certain carriers may only support updates on a quarterly basis.

²The number of device models with quarterly security patch updates is subject to change and will be reviewed on a regular basis.
