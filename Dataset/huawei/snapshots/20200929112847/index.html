Huawei EMUI/Magic UI security updates

About EMUI/Magic UI Security Patch Updates

  • Huawei will introduce a monthly and quarterly security patch update programme for the following device models.
  • The monthly and quarterly security patch update programmes include patches for the Android OS, and patches for Huawei devices released by Huawei.
  • The release time of security patches may vary depending on the region and device model.

Current monthly security patch updates will be released for the following device models¹

HUAWEI P series: P40, P40 Pro, P40 Pro+, P30 Pro, P30

HUAWEI Mate series: Mate 30, Mate 30 pro, Mate 30 Pro 5G, Mate 20 X, Mate 20 X 5G, Mate 20 Pro, Mate 20, Mate 20 RS, Mate Xs

HONOR series: Honor 30, Honor 30 Pro, Honor View30, Honor View30 Pro, Honor 20, Honor 20 Pro, Honor View20

Current quarterly security patch updates will be released for the following device models²

HUAWEI P series: P40 lite, P40 lite 5G, P40 lite E, P smart+ 2019, P smart 2020, P smart Z, P30 lite, P Smart 2019, P smart Pro, P smart S

HUAWEI Mate series: Mate 20 lite

HUAWEI Y series: Y9 Prime 2019, Y9 2019, Y7 2019, Y6 2019, Y6 Pro 2019, Y5 2019, Y MAX, HUAWEI Y9s, HUAWEI Y8s, HUAWEI Y6s, HUAWEI Y8P, HUAWEI Y7P, HUAWEI Y6P, HUAWEI Y5P, HUAWEI Y5 lite

HONOR series: Honor 30S, Honor 20i, Honor 20e, Honor 10i, Honor 10 Lite, Honor 9X, Honor 9X Pro, Honor 9X Lite, Honor 9A, Honor 9C, Honor 9S, Honor 8A, Honor 8X, Honor 8X Max, Honor 8S, Honor 8C, Honor 7S

HUAWEI nova series: nova 7, nova 7i, nova 7 SE, nova 5T, nova 4, nova 3, nova Lite 3, nova 3i

Tablets: HUAWEI MatePad Pro, M6(8.4" & 10.8"), T5 (10.1"), M5 lite (10.1" & 8.0")

¹The number of device models with monthly security patch updates is subject to change. Certain carriers may only support updates on a quarterly basis.

²The number of device models with quarterly security patch updates is subject to change and will be reviewed on a regular basis.
