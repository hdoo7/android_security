Huawei EMUI/Magic UI security updates

About EMUI/Magic UI Security Patch Updates

  • Huawei will introduce a monthly and quarterly security patch update programme for the following device models.
  • The monthly and quarterly security patch update programmes include patches for the Android OS, and patches for Huawei devices released by Huawei.
  • The release time of security patches may vary depending on the region and device model.

Current monthly security patch updates will be released for the following device models¹

HUAWEI Mate series: HUAWEI Mate 40 Pro, HUAWEI Mate 30, HUAWEI Mate 30 Pro, HUAWEI Mate 30 Pro 5G, HUAWEI Mate 20 X (5G),HUAWEI Mate 20 Pro, HUAWEI Mate Xs

HUAWEI P series: HUAWEI P40, HUAWEI P40 Pro, HUAWEI P40 Pro+, HUAWEI P30, HUAWEI P30 lite New Edition, HUAWEI P30 Pro

HONOR series: HONOR 20, HONOR 20 Pro, HONOR View 20

Current quarterly security patch updates will be released for the following device models²

HUAWEI P series: HUAWEI P40 lite, HUAWEI P40 lite 5G, HUAWEI P40 lite E, HUAWEI P30 lite, HUAWEI P smart 2021, HUAWEI P smart 2020, HUAWEI P smart 2019, HUAWEI P smart S,HUWEI P smart Pro

HUAWEI nova series: HUAWEI nova 7 5G, HUAWEI nova 7 SE 5G, HUAWEI nova 7i, HUAWEI nova 5T,HUAWEI nova lite 3+

HUAWEI Y series: HUAWEI Y5p, HUAWEI Y6p, HUAWEI Y6s, HUAWEI Y6 2019,HUAWEI Y6 Pro 2019, HUAWEI Y7a,HUAWEI Y7p, HUAWEI Y8p, HUAWEI Y8s, HUAWEI Y9 Prime 2019, HUAWEI Y9a, HUAWEI Y9s

HONOR series: HONOR 30, HONOR 30 Pro+, HONOR 30i, HONOR 30S, HONOR View 30 Pro, HONOR 20e, HONOR 20i, HONOR 20S, HONOR 20 lite, HONOR 10i, HONOR 9A, HONOR 9C, HONOR 9S, HONOR 9X,HONOR 10X, HONOR 9X lite,HONOR 10X lite, HONOR 9X Pro, HONOR 8A, HONOR 8S, HONOR 8A Pro

Tablets: HUAWEI MediaPad M5 lite 8, HUAWEI MatePad Pro, HUAWEI MediaPad M6, HUAWEI MatePad , HUAWEI MatePad T , HUAWEI MatePad T 10s, HUAWEI MatePad T 10, HONOR Pad V6

¹The number of device models with monthly security patch updates is subject to change. Certain carriers may only support updates on a quarterly basis.
